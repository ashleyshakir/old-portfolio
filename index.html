about me

Hello World! My name is Ashley Shakir. I am a Software Engineer with a love for technology and education. I graduated with a B.A. in Computer Science a little over 3 years ago and kicked my career off teaching elementary students how to code their own video games! It is such a rewarding experience to see young learners have "ah ha!" moments when they understand a new coding concept and add a new skill to their programming toolbox. Similar to my students, I continue to have "ah ha!" moments as I level up my skills in front end and back end development.
